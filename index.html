QR Code Generator — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28

TL;DR   This app collects nothing, sends nothing, and has no servers. Everything stays on your phone.

1. Data we collect

None. QR Code Generator is fully offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDK, and no third-party network calls.

2. Data stored on your device

Your generated QR history (URLs, Wi-Fi credentials, contacts, etc. that you input) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device only. It never leaves your phone. You can clear it any time from the History screen.

3. Permissions

  • Photo / media access (only on save): used solely to write the generated QR PNG to your gallery when you tap "Save". The app never reads existing media.

4. Third-party services

None. No SDKs, no analytics, no crash reporting, no ads.

5. Children

This app is safe for all ages. It does not request any personal information and does not transmit any data.

6. Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: laughaholicpro@gmail.com
